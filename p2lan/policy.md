# Privacy Policy for P2lan

**Effective Date:** March 2026

**1. Nature of the Service (Zero Server Storage)**

P2lan is a cross-platform, peer-to-peer (P2P) file-sharing application designed for Local Area Networks (LAN). We prioritize your privacy: **we do not collect, store, intercept, or monitor the files you transfer.** All data transmission occurs directly between the connected devices. Your files are never uploaded to or hosted on any developer or third-party servers.

**2. Required Permissions and Device Data**

To function correctly, P2lan requires certain device permissions. Here is exactly how they are used locally:
* **Local Network Access:** Required strictly to utilize LAN discovery protocols to find other devices running P2lan on the same network, and to establish direct P2P connections (including bridge mode functionality). 
* **Storage Access (Read/Write):** Required locally on your device to read the files you explicitly choose to send, and to save the files you receive from peers.

**3. Analytics and Tracking**

P2lan operates as a decentralized, offline-first tool. We do not track your network topology, transfer history, IP addresses, or personal information. We do not use any tracking SDKs or advertising modules.

**4. Security Disclaimer**

While P2lan facilitates direct file transfers, the security of the transmission depends on the safety of your local network environment. Users are responsible for ensuring they are connected to a trusted network when sharing sensitive files.

**5. Contact**

For inquiries regarding this Privacy Policy or the open-source architecture of the application, please open an issue on the official P2lan GitHub repository.
