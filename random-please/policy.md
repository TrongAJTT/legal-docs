# Privacy Policy for Random Please

**Effective Date:** March 2026

**1. No Data Collection**
Random Please is a local utility application designed to generate random outcomes. We respect your privacy and explicitly state that **we do not collect, store, track, transmit, or share any personal information, usage data, or device identifiers.**

**2. Local Processing**
All core functionalities—including coin flips, dice rolls, card draws, and other randomizations—are computed entirely locally on your device. Your data and interaction history never leave your device and are never sent to any external servers.

**3. Third-Party Services**
Random Please operates independently. We do not integrate third-party analytics trackers, crash reporters, or advertising SDKs that harvest user data. 

**4. Changes to This Policy**
If we update this policy to reflect new features, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this document.

**5. Contact**
If you have any questions regarding our privacy practices, please contact the developer via the official GitHub repository.
