# Privacy Policy for Heta - Tab Helper

**Effective Date:** March 23, 2026

**1. Zero Data Collection**

Heta - Tab Helper is a client-side browser extension designed to help you manage your open tabs and URLs efficiently. We are committed to your privacy: **we do not collect, store, track, transmit, or share your browsing history, open tabs, URLs, or any personal data.**

**2. Why We Need Specific Permissions**

To function as a comprehensive tab manager, Heta requires the following permissions, which are used strictly locally on your device:
* **`tabs` & `<all_urls>`:** Required to read the URLs and titles of your currently open tabs so the extension can group, manage, or close them based on your commands. This data is processed in real-time and is never saved outside your browser.
* **`declarativeNetRequest`:** Used locally to manage and route URLs effectively according to the extension's core features.
* **`storage`:** Used solely to save your personal extension settings and preferences locally on your machine.

**3. External Servers and Third Parties**

Heta operates 100% offline within your browser. It does not communicate with any external servers, nor does it include any third-party analytics or advertising trackers.

**4. Changes to This Policy**

If our privacy practices change, we will update the "Effective Date" of this document.

**5. Contact Us**

For any privacy-related questions or to review the open-source code, please visit the official Heta GitHub repository.
